About TSA

The Technology Student Association (TSA) is a national organization dedicated to promoting science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM) education. Our club participates in TSA competitions where members compete in various technology and engineering events at regional and state levels. Our team combines creativity, problem-solving, and technical expertise to develop innovative solutions and showcase our skills.

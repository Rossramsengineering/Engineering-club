Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to complete an order? expand_more
Most orders are completed within 1-2 weeks depending on the complexity of the project. Custom orders may take longer. We'll provide you with an estimated timeline when you place your order.
Can I customize products? expand_more
Absolutely! Many of our products can be customized. You can upload your design or provide specifications for LED signs, 3D prints, and engraved items. Contact us for custom requests.
What payment methods do you accept? expand_more
We accept payment on pickup. You can pay via cash or card when you collect your order. For large or custom projects, we can discuss payment arrangements.
Do you offer bulk discounts? expand_more
Yes! We offer discounts for bulk orders. If you're interested in ordering multiple items, please contact us directly to discuss pricing and availability.
Can I request a refund? expand_more
Refunds are available within 7 days of purchase if the product is unopened and in its original condition. For custom orders, please contact us to discuss your specific situation.
